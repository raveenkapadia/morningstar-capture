Common Questions

Before you visit

Do I need a referral? +
No. You can book directly with any of our specialists without a GP referral. Simply select your speciality when booking.
Is my insurance accepted? +
We accept all major UAE health plans including Daman (enhanced and basic), AXA, Allianz, Metlife, Cigna, and most corporate insurance policies. Call us to verify your specific plan.
How soon can I get an appointment? +
Most patients can be seen same-day or next-day. Urgent medical concerns are prioritised ‚Äî call us directly and we will accommodate you.
Are consultations available in Arabic? +
Yes. Many of our doctors and all of our patient services team are fully fluent in Arabic. Please request an Arabic-speaking doctor when booking.
